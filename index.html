Fuel & Duty – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Fuel & Duty is intended for internal company use only. The app does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information or user data.

  • The app does not connect or communicate with external servers.
  • The app does not collect analytics, identifiers, or usage data.
  • The app requires a one-time access password to enable operation. This password is hard-coded within the app and verified locally on the device. It is not transmitted, stored, or linked to any individual user.
  • The app does not request permissions such as location, contacts, camera, or photos.
